DIY Builder Checklist
Builder Tips
🎯
Start with your build prompt. Paste it exactly as written into your platform of choice. Don't rewrite it — the prompt is engineered to get the best output.
🔁
Iterate in small steps. Get one feature working before adding the next. A simple tool that works beats a complex one that doesn't.
🧪
Test it yourself first. Use your own tool as if you were a client before sharing it. What's confusing? What's missing? Fix those first.
💬
Stuck? Post in the community. Share your prompt and describe what's not working. Someone has been there.
🚀
Done doesn't mean perfect. Launch when it does the main thing well. You can improve it after real feedback — which is worth more than another week of tweaking alone.
