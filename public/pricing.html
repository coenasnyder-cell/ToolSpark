Common questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal whenever you like. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What happens when I run out of credits?
You'll see a "not enough credits" message when you try to run a tool. Credits reset on your next monthly renewal date.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Yes — changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Upgrades are prorated immediately.
Is this the same as the Certification Program?
No — the Certification Program is a separate one-time purchase. These plans are for ongoing platform access and AI tool credits.
